Four steps, one lifetime

Nothing about this is fast.

Every Iron Legacy pan passes through the same four stages our foundry has used since the first pour. No shortcuts, no pre-fab molds, no skipping the season.

01

Smelt

Raw iron ore is melted at 1,400°C until it runs like water.

02

Pour

Molten iron is poured into sand molds, one pan at a time.

03

Temper

Cooled slowly, then ground and polished smooth by hand.

04

Season

Three coats of oil, baked in until the iron turns black and slick.
